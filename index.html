Company Profile

Introduction

Rommelo Technologies was incorporated in the year 2016 as an electrical and civil engineering solutions provider. The company has been successful inexecution of construction of electric power lines both overhead and underground and laying of fiber optic(data)cables to utility companies. With an experienced team of professionals in the field, modernized equipment and financial capability, we have been able to meet our project timelines in adherence to quality standards..

Excutive summary

Rommelo Technologies is a licensed electrical and building works services contractor under category NCA 8. We have been able to secure a standard and well – positioned office facility at Bevkesh House, Ruiru Town along Thika road. We are an electrical contractor services company that is set to compete in the highly competitive electrical contractor services industry not only in Kenya, but also throughout Africa.

IMPLEMENTATION, INSTALLATION AND TRAINING

Rommelo Technologies provide project management, planning and implementation, including site supervision, installation and commissioning for projects of all sizes and complexity. Our technical management teams can advise the client on the best selection of products and equipment available from a wide range of suppliers. Our periodic and preventative maintenance service is based on our experience of industry standards and manufacturers recommendations.
