POLICIES

QUALITY MANAGEMENT POLICY

Rommelo Technologies Limited provides a quality system that delivers to our customers and clients value added benefits, by developing long term relationships to better understand customer needs and ensure both suppliers and sub-contractors live up to the standards required.

In line with our QMS procedures, a quality plan will be developed for the project which will detail the critical check and hold points for test and inspection for the scope of our supply. All quality plans are site specific and are produced in close conjunction with clientâ€™s specific requirements. Site Management and Supervision apply the Quality Plan and maintain a professional approach to quality and disciplined record keeping. On site Quality meetings, periodic audits, and project Close Out Meetings are held to ensure the project quality objectives are reviewed and met. Customer and Client feedback is welcome at all times, so that we can better understand how we are performing in the eyes of the customer and do our very best to ensure we provide a competitive advantage due to our standards of quality and our reputation in the marketplace.

ENVIRONMENTAL CONSERVASION POLICY

Our policy at Rommelo Technologies limited is to recognize our responsibility to protect the environment and minimize any adverse environmental impact of its activities, as far as is safe, practicable and economical, with the objective of being incident free. It is also our practice to identify areas of opportunity to develop an environmentally sustainable business. This is carried out at an overall business management, and project-by-project level upon review of project specific environmental and sustainability aspects and impacts.
