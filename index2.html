Executive Summary

Executive

Rommelo Technologies is a licensed electrical and building works services contractor under category NCA 8. We have been able to secure a standard and well – positioned office facility at Bevkesh House, Ruiru Town along Thika road. We are an electrical contractor services company that is set to compete in the highly competitive electrical contractor services industry not only in Kenya, but also throughout Africa..

Rommelo Technologies executes electrical work at sites such as construction of powerlines on overhead and underground ranges, fiber optic cable installation etc. Basically, we provide services such as electric power and systems installation and servicing, telecommunications installation and servicing, fire and security system installation and servicing, electronic control system installation and servicing.

Our business goal is to become one of the leading electrical contractor service companies in Kenya and we make sure that we do all we can to compete favorably with leaders in the industry.

Our workers are selected from a pool of certified and highly experienced electrical technicians and tradesmen (electricians) with different area of expertise as it relates to our service offerings in and around the country. We make sure that we take all the members of our workforce through the required trainings that position them to meet the expectation of the company and to compete with leading electrical contractor services companies.

At Rommelo Technologies, our client’s best interest always come first, and everything we do is guided by our values and professional ethics. We ensure that we hold ourselves accountable to the highest standards by meeting our client’s needs precisely and completely. We cultivate a working environment that provides a human, sustainable approach to earning a living, and living in our world, for all our partners, employees and for our clients.
