About Rommelo Technologies

Introduction

Rommelo Technologies was incorporated in the year 2016 as an electrical and civil engineering solutions provider.

The company has been successful inexecution of construction of electric power lines both overhead and underground and laying of fiber optic(data)cables to utility companies. With an experienced team of professionals in the field, modernized equipment and financial capability, we have been able to meet our project timelines in adherence to quality standards.

Vision

To be the leading electrical and civil Engineering provider. Through provision of quality, cost effective services to our customers.

Mission

Enriching lives through engineering and energy solutions. Jamwe limited has its central mission through teamwork, the carrying out of any undertaking safely, professionally and with excellence within the industry.
