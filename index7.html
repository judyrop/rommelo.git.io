IMPLEMENTATION, INSTALLATION AND TRAINING

Rommelo Technologies provide project management, planning and implementation, including site supervision, installation and commissioning for projects of all sizes and complexity. Our technical management teams can advise the client on the best selection of products and equipment available from a wide range of suppliers. Our periodic and preventative maintenance service is based on our experience of industry standards and manufacturers recommendations.

Rommelo Technologies provides all necessary installation services including:

  • Topographical surveys, setting and marking out.
  • Cable trench excavation and construction, pit and duct systems, foundation construction
  • Construction of electric power lines both overhead and underground.
  • Fiber optic cable installations.
  • Training
  • Testing and Commissioning
